## Introduction
 
This is the start of a post series with my English study notes based on a previous post when we designed a plan to improve English to work fluently—both writing and speaking.
 
Not C2 level, but enough to work remotely without any communication problems.
 
In my case, as a software engineer.
 
Each step of the plan has one post here as notes for study and accountability of this process.
 
Each big step includes smaller steps:
1. Write the entire post without extra resources, only my words.
2. Use AI (in my case Claude) for feedback about my writing (words, grammar, different ways to say the same thing, etc.).
3. List all my mistakes to study later.
4. **Stand up reading** with screen recording and audio.
5. **Stand up without reading** — just talk about the topic.

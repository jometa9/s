Another one I built. This one isn't new — I made MonchoOps a few months ago, I just never wrote about it here. It's free, it runs on your own machine, and it lives here:

- Landing: [jometa9.github.io/MONCHOOPS-LANDING](https://jometa9.github.io/MONCHOOPS-LANDING/)
- Windows, macOS, or a Chrome extension — all the download buttons are on the landing.

What it does: scrape leads on Instagram — from profiles, posts, hashtags, locations — and then send them cold DMs at scale, across many accounts at once. All from one app.

Here's the part I care about. Doing this the normal way means stitching together two or three different tools. One to scrape the list. One to clean the duplicates. One to send the messages. Each with its own login, its own subscription, its own monthly bill. And you spend half your time copy-pasting between them.

MonchoOps is all of it in one place. You pull the leads, it dedupes them for you, it can warm each one up first — a follow, a story view, a like — and then you send from 20+ accounts in parallel, with up to 20 message variants so every DM isn't the exact same copy-paste. It keeps the full history, so you never message the same person twice.

It runs locally. No account, no credit card, nothing to pay.

So if you do outreach on Instagram, or you've been paying for three tools to do one job, this replaces all of them.

Scrape. DM. Done.
